Fun Climb

What is Fun Climb?

Fun Climb is an arcade game, developed by a small team of University of Tabriz's students. It is a fun endless game full of upgrades and skills to unlock with various characters and skins .

In Fun Climb, players have to control a character who is climbing a mountain by a rope that shifts color from time to time. The game is still in the development and data gathering stage.

As the founder of University of Tabriz's Innovation center's Game Lab, I founded this team of eager students who wanted to work on game development for their externship course and started working on this project as the project manager, programmer, and designer.

Developer: Finite Loop Interactive
Platforms: iOS, Android
Genres: Arcade, Casual, Endless
