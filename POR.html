Pursuit of Redemption

What is Pursuit of Redemption?

Pursuit of Redemption is a story-driven puzzle game with a variety of special platformer levels, such as an exciting maze level, fun and engaging jetpack levels, and more.

Pursuit of Redemption is a touching single-player experience, with deep narrative flow, unique artstyle and challenging levels.

We started this project in 2016 as a team of 5 and I have been the director, writer and game designer of the game. Pursuit of Redemption is planned to be released in 2020 for PC and Nintendo Switch.

Developer: VOiD Games
Platforms: PC, Nintendo Switch
Release Date: 2020
Genres: Platformer, Puzzle, Text Adventure
