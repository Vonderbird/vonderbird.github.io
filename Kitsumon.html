What is Kitsumon?

"Kitsumon" is an NFT game about collecting, breeding, and caring for adorable Kitsu pets. From professions like Farming, Fishing, Crafting and more, and an in-depth breeding system, all the way to MOBA PvP modes and land acquisition, Kitsumon has various gameplays, ways to earn, and Quests to get immeresed in!

The world of Kitsumon was first populated by 20 primordial Kitsus, forming the Generation 0 of the population. Every Kitsu has its own Digital DNA with 44 individual Genes, and you will be able to breed, train and battle them in MOBA battles.

I am the Lead Game Designer of Kitsumon, and I work on gameplay systems, level design, mechanics, game's economy, Specifications and levelling system, quests, tutorials, UI, and more.

Platforms: PC, Mobile, Web
