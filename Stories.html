Rhymeless Tales of a Wingless Bird

I am a writer.
I write and I write and I write.

I have been writing short stories, novels and screenplays for over 12 years now. I started back in 2008 with writing a gamescript called "Destination".

Since that time, I have taken various courses, read numerous books and had a lot of studies.
I have written multiple stories in different genres over the years for various projects such as video games, animations, documentaries, etc. I am currently working on my first Full-Length novel called "Freedoom".
"Rhymeless Tales of a Wingless Bird" is a collection of 10 short stories, depicting tales from alternate worlds, each with their own unique subject and unexpectable and exciting twists, and sharply written characters.
